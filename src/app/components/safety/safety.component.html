Coronavirus Safety Tips

What is Coronavirus

Coronavirus disease 2019, or COVID-19, is a disease that can cause what doctors call a respiratory tract infection. It can affect your upper respiratory tract (sinuses, nose, and throat) or lower respiratory tract (windpipe and lungs).

What Are the Symptoms of COVID-19?

Early symptoms include a fever, dry cough or fatigue. The virus can lead to pneumonia, respiratory failure, septic shock, and death. If youâ€™re exposed and infected, symptoms can show up in as few as 2 days or as many as 14.

How do you prevent the spread?

You should wash your hands often with soap and water as this kills viruses. Practice social distancing because you can have and spread the virus without knowing it. Don't touch your face as the virus can live on surfaces you touch.

Here are some safety tips

Wash your hands for at least 20 seconds

Disinfect surfaces around your home and work

Sneeze or cough? Cover your mouth or wear a mask
